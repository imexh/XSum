About Us

“X-Sum” is a joint project organized by the Model United Nations Club and Green Army of Sri Lanka Technological Campus (SLTC), in order to provide an opportunity for all the SLTC undergraduates to enhance their knowledge and awareness of Sustainable Development Goals (SDGS). Accordingly, we are conducting a summit under the topic SDGS. The summit will consist of several forums organized by MUN Club of SLTC, Green Army of SLTC and a few other clubs of the campus who are willing to take part. Each forum will have approximately 15 delegates and they will be recruited through registrations. There will be no language restrictions for the summit. English, Sinhala and Tamil speakers are equally encouraged. The summit will be open for all SLTC undergraduates who are enthusiastic and interested in the subject.

Green Army of SLTC

The Green Army of Sri Lanka Technological Campus was launched on April 6, 2019.The SLTC Green Army, which is made up of approximately 300 volunteers who care about the environment, now plays an important role at the institution.

Their goal is to increase the country's forest cover by launching environmental conservation projects, wildlife conservation projects, formulating programs for Sri Lanka's agricultural realization, and continuing to conduct continuous research on the need to increase country's forest cover as the university's first committee made up of researchers and developers.

MUN Club of SLTC

Sri Lanka Technological Campus Model United Nations Club seeks to further the mission of Sri Lanka Technological Campus by:

  • Enabling students to contribute to the richness of cultural diversity.
  • Encouraging leaders who will make positive contributions to an increasingly interdependent and multicultural world.
  • Fostering reasoned discussion of contemporary issues.
  • Instilling an eagerness to embrace the challenges of a more global future.
  • Creating a society that is good in public speaking, negotiating and debating skills.
  • Developing self-confidence and decision-making abilities.
